Driving While License Suspended Cases in Judge Farr's Courtroom

42% of people charged with Driving While License Suspended With Knowledge are convicted. 44% of these cases are dismissed, and 14% of people receive a withhold of adjudication, meaning that while they may receive a penalty for the crime, they do not receive a conviction on their record.

Do people hire an attorney for Driving While License Suspended cases in Judge Farr's courtroom?

Only 13% of people charged with Driving While License Suspended With Knowledge hire a private attorney in Judge Farr's courtroom. 15% go with the Public Defender. 72% decide to handle their cases on their own without an attorney.

Having a private attorney is not a clear advantage in Judge Farr's courtroom for these cases.
